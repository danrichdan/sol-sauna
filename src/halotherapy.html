What is Halotherapy/Salt Therapy?

The term “Halotherapy” comes from “halo”, the Greek word for salt. Simply put, Halotherapy is the use of salt vapor to treat respiratory ailments, skin irritations, and combat mental lethargy.

Past and Present

For centuries, monks used naturally-occurring salt caverns to treat respiratory ailments. Today, manmade Saltrooms recreate salt caverns, with one huge improvement over nature: in a Halotherapy room, a salt vaporizer/halogenerator pumps dry aerosol salt vapor into the air, so you can breathe it deep into your lungs. Think of it as a netty pot for your lungs.

Salt is known to be:

  • anti-bacterial
  • mucolytic (loosens mucus & phlegm)
  • anti-inflammatory

Sol Sauna Salt Lounge

At Sol Sauna and Salt Lounge, members enjoy a 45 minute session in either a private or community room. Come in, sit, relax, and soak in the salt.

Arrive 5 minutes before the salt session, because once the halogenerator starts, the room will be closed during the duration. Wear comfortable clothing, clean socks (mandatory) and hair kept back. The room is kept around 68F for the session.
